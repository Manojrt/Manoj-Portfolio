About Me

Hello! My name is Manoj! , and I am a dedicated front-end developer who excels in creating engaging digital experiences. My extensive skill set includes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I take pride in converting design concepts into visually stunning and intuitive web solutions. I have an eye for aesthetics and focus on user-centric design principles to ensure my products are both appealing and easy to use. I am proficient in using modern CSS frameworks such as Tailwind CSS and Bootstrap, which expedite development processes and ensure consistency across projects.

Though I specialize in front-end technologies, I have a basic understanding of React.js and Redux toolkit and I am willing to learn more about these frameworks. I began my career as a UX-UI designer and have experience working on various projects.

In addition to front-end development, I am well-versed in Java and SQL, which enables me to seamlessly integrate with backend systems and manage databases.
